Privacy Policy

Last Updated: April 1, 2026

1. Introduction

PowerLink Plumbing ("we," "our," or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how your personal information is collected, used, and disclosed by PowerLink Plumbing.

2. Information We Collect

We collect information that you provide directly to us when you:

  • Create an account or register as a provider
  • Place an order for supplies or book a plumber
  • Contact our customer support
  • Sign up for our newsletter

3. Cookies and Tracking

We use cookies to maintain your session, remember your cart items, and analyze site traffic. You can choose to opt out of non-essential tracking via our cookie consent banner.

4. Marketing Communications

If you have provided your email address, we may send you marketing communications. You can opt out of these at any time by:

  • Using the "Opt out" checkbox in the cookie banner
  • Clicking the "Unsubscribe" link in any marketing email
  • Updating your preferences in your account settings

5. Data Security

We implement industry-standard security measures, including bcrypt password hashing and SSL encryption, to protect your data.

6. Contact Us

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy, please contact us at support@powerlink.com.
